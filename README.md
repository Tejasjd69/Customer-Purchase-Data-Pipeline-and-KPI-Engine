Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis
📌 Business Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze customer shopping behavior and identify key business drivers that influence revenue, customer retention, and purchasing decisions. The insights derived from this analysis are intended to support strategic planning, targeted marketing, and customer lifecycle optimization.

📂 Dataset Overview

3,900 customer purchase transactions

18 business and behavioral attributes, including demographics, product categories, discounts, shipping type, subscriptions, and customer ratings

Data reflects real-world retail purchase behavior

🔍 Business Questions Addressed

Which customer segments contribute the most to revenue?

How do discounts impact high-value customer spending?

Does shipping preference influence purchase value?

What is the revenue impact of customer subscriptions?

Which products receive the highest customer satisfaction?

⚙️ Methodology

Cleaned and prepared data using Python (pandas, NumPy)

Handled missing values using statistically appropriate imputation

Created derived features such as age groups and purchase frequency

Used SQL (PostgreSQL) to perform structured analysis and segmentation

Conducted exploratory data analysis to identify trends and patterns

📊 Key Business Insights

Female customers generated slightly higher total revenue than male customers

High-spending customers who used discounts remained profitable, indicating effective discount strategies

Express shipping customers spent approximately 12% more per transaction

Subscription customers showed higher average spending and repeat purchase rates

Certain product categories consistently received high customer ratings
